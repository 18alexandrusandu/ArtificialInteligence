Welcome to Escape from prison game



        

In the Escape Jail game , you are a wrongfully accused prisoner that desperately needs to
escape from the grid-like prison. In order to escape you need to find the only key of the
prison and the exit.The problem is that nobody knows where they are. Also the prison is not
a easy place to escape from. There are a handful of different dangers and you do not know
where they are. But no worry, you made friends that can help you with that and you managed
to take with you your trusty friend mace4. If you are able to find your friends letters that
contain secret messages, avoid dangers find the key and door
you win your right to leave the prison and get to freedom.

The prison is represented by a 8x8 grid. You are represented by an orange square. You can move up,
down,left and right using key arrows, messages will inform mace4 about where the dangers are and it
will prevent you from ending tragically
